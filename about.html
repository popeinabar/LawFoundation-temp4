About Us

[org name] is non-for-profit organisation, carrying out grassroot level interventions since [year] under the Criminal Justice Fellowship Programme of [uni] registered as a Trust in [year]. Our core work revolves around enabling socio-legal aid, networking, training, rehabilitation, re-integration and advocacy with respect to the custodial, institutional, legal and constitutional rights of socio-economically marginalized communities. To this end, permission to work with the criminal justice system and interact with persons detained or confined in prisons and government residential institutions in [place] has been obtained from the [position] [names]

Mission

To extend socio-legal aid to disadvantaged communities, groups and individuals; enabling equal access to constitutional rights.

Vision

To End Structural Inequality towards the Disadvantaged Population and Promote Fair, Just & Inclusive Society for All.

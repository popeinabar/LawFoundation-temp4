Gurudev Nanda
Social Worker
Gurudev Nanda has a master degree in Sociology and Social Anthropology from TISS Guwahati campus. He engaged himself with the organization in 2022. As a passionate, active and young person, his perspective brings a unique value to the organization's work. He has now visited a number of jails in Bihar and, using his great interpersonal skills, has counselled a number of prisoners inside jail. Gurudev also pays visit to the prisoner's family at their residence and provide them any assistance they require including family counselling, social advocacy and emergency support. He also works on the Sustainable Mental Health Project within the prison, which aims to protect the inmates from mental illness.
